Welcome to my Portfolio

Growing up in the culturally vibrant city of Guayaquil, Ecuador, I was privileged to be surrounded by art, music, theater, literature and poetry from a very young age. This background engendered in me a lifelong passion for experiencing and creating art. Today I am a graphic designer based in Charlotte,NC.


I have advanced skills in CS Suite, Logic, and different types of CMS and over 15 years of experience as a graphic designer in both Ecuador and North Carolina.


Currently I work for Britax Child Safety as Senior Graphic Designer and as a freelance for local clients such as Centralina Council of Governments, Carolinas Care Partnership, the Latin American Women’s Association, among others.
